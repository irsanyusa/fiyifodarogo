 Posted on August 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  396 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

A 25-year-old student from France is gaining popularity with his Instagram account Morphy_me , where he cleverly takes features from two high profile figures and creates a hybrid portrait.

His work has received attention from celebrities such as Gigi Hadid, Mark Hamill, Doutzen Kroes, and more.

ADVERTISEMENT

On the Morphy_me Instagram page, there are just short of a thousand photos, totaling hundreds of hours in editing time.

Some highlights include facial mashups of Gal Gadot and Brie Larson, Lady Gaga and Scarlett Johansson, and Chris Hemsworth and Jason Momoa.

The artist, called Ben, also sometimes takes on an unusual challenge he recently morphed the faces of celebrity couple Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively.

"I already did some morphs before, just for me, and thought it would be cool to share them. It has been really unexpected to see this account becoming big," the artist told the Mail Online .

"It takes me around an hour to do a morph. A bit longer than when I started, because I now try to do them as detailed as I can. The way I do them is still my secret."

He added that his favourite is probably the morph of Lana Del Rey and Natalie Portman.

"In my opinion we can really see both of them at the same time," he said. "I admire those two women, and I love this photo."
