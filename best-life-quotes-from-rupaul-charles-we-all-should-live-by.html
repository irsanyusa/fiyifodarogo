As the most famous supermodel of the world, RuPaul Charles surely knows a thing or two about life. The drag queen is beyond any doubt a wise person, proven by her incredible wisdom.

Here are some of the best life quotes from RuPaul Charles that may be worth remembering.

RuPaul Charles’ Best Life Quotes We All Should Live By

1. “If You Can’t Love Yourself, How in The Hell are You Gonna Love Somebody Else?”

This line is perhaps the most famous quote from RuPaul, and it’s no wonder why. Self-love is an essential part of living, but we all know by experience that it’s easier said than done. Nonetheless, it pays knowing that you can’t give what you don’t have. In the same vein, how can love fully if you don’t learn to love yourself?

2. “You Are Not Your Body. You are Actually an Extension of the Power That Created The Whole Universe.”

With the effortless ability of the society to set unrealistic body standards, we hope you find comfort in these words. Indeed, our size will never define us, our reality, our dreams. It’s not our bones that make us special, but rather our character.

3. “That is The Key to Navigating This Life— Don’t Take it Too Seriously. That’s When The Party Begins.”

When pressure gets too high, one must remember that it’s important to loosen up. In order to keep going, a person should know how and when to have fun. Thus, when the time comes that life asks you to be serious, you know that you didn’t deprive yourself of the happiness you deserve.

4. “Negativity is Basically Laziness. It Takes a lot of Hard Work to Remain Positive, But Positivity Always Pays Off.”

We understand, negativity can really suck up all the bliss you’ve strived hard to be in. Be that as it may, it’s worth remembering that it isn’t good to entertain negativity for a long time. That said, it’s best to find the silver lining out of every situation to serve as motivation for the future.

5. “To Win at Life And to Win in This Competition, You Have to be Willing to Die a Thousand Deaths And be Reborn a Thousand times.”

This is perhaps the most fabulous way of saying that growing is not comfortable and convenient. Hence, every individual should be humble enough to acknowledge mistakes and change for the better. Indeed, the sweet taste of victory can only be seized with a number of meaningful losses.

6. “Unless They’re Paying Your Bills, Pay Them B*tches No Mind!”

Can we get an amen? This requires no deep comprehension. You control your own life. You are the anchor, so pay no attention to people who don’t matter.
