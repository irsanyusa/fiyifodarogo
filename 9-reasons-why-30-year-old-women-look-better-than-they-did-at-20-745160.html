8 Reasons Why 30-Year-Old Women Look Better Than They Did at 20

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  566 words  |  Martina Birk

Modern 30-year-old women often look so stunning that it is easy to confuse them for 20-year-olds. They are just as beautiful and young. And it is not just about the appearance, but it’s also about their behavior, their voice, and their body language. Women with a 10-year age difference look as if they are from the same generation. Maybe it’s because they really are?

We at Bright Side wondered what 20-year-olds and 30-year-olds have in common and why older women often look better than when they were young.

They don’t care about age or other things as much.

As strange as it seems, age actually helps us to be more relaxed about many things, including the age itself and public opinion. 30-year-old people are more confident and are less worried about what people think of them. This makes them more interesting and attractive people.

They follow the fashion trends.

Very often, it is really hard to tell a 20-year old woman from a 30-year old: modern women follow the same makeup and clothing trends. At 20 or 30, they can use the same makeup, wear the same jeans, T-shirts, and whatever is trendy that season.

They can afford expensive things and services.

By the age of 30, most people already understand what they want to do in their lives, and they have some business reputation. And even if they don’t, they have some experience which is extremely valuable. Having more money than just what they make from a scholarship or a part-time job allows them to spend it on themselves: buying expensive clothes, and visiting beauty salons and cosmetologists.

They have found their style and they keep looking.

In a way, 30 years old is the golden age in terms of image. By this time, a woman already has everything: she understands what suits her and what doesn’t, she has the courage to experiment with her appearance, and she has the money to experiment with experts and use only the most high-quality materials.

They know what they want.

People in their 20s have a lot of interests and hobbies, are looking for their professional path, and fall in love chaotically. When they are 30, they have a much better understanding of what they want from their lives, and they do everything they can to reach their goals. And self-confidence is always attractive.

They’ve overcome their insecurities.

Or they just learned how to live with them. Every person has their own insecurities to deal with: stereotypes about themselves, self-doubt, nervous breakdowns. When a person just starts living on their own, their background may let them down at pretty much any point. But by the age of 30, they have either dealt with their problems or have learned to live with them, which makes any woman more attractive.

They stopped eating bad food.

Of course, we can’t say that 30-year-olds don’t eat fast food at all, but they definitely try to reduce the amount and improve the overall quality of their diets. Older women think about what they eat, and they cook more often than younger girls. This approach has a positive effect on their skin, their mood, and their overall look.

They realize that they won’t be young forever.

When a woman is 20 years old, she feels like she will always look this way: her body will be strong and healthy, her skin will be toned. This is why 20-year-olds have the time to do nothing for a year or 2. But when you are 30, you realize that the future is closer than it seems and that it is time to start taking care of your body and mind. 30-year-old women are more determined to stay healthy and young and this attracts a lot of people.

The most important thing to remember is that any age has its advantages, and we just need to learn to enjoy them. What are the advantages of your age?
