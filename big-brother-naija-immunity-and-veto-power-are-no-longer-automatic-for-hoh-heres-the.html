Immunity and veto power are no longer automatic for HOH, here's the twist

 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  162 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

For the first time in the history of #BBNaija, whoever emerges the HOH won't be guaranteed immunity and the veto power to save a nominated housemate and replace with another.

Instead of automatic immunity, whoever wins the Head of House title will be required to pick one out of four cards, which will determine if he or she is immune from possible eviction or has the power to save and replace.

For this week's Head of House title, Nina, Miracle, Tobi and Cee-C have qualified to take part in the final challenge, which is set to take place this evening.
