Daniella Karagach and Pasha Pashkov are beginning new year with a celebration of love. The Dancing With The Stars real-life couple recently celebrated 15 years of being together.

Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov Celebrate 15 Years Together

On Instagram, Karagach paid tribute to his husband amid their remarkable milestone. The Dancing With The Stars season 30 winner uploaded a carousel, showing some of their throwback snaps and some photos with their daughter Nikita.

“& what a life we’ve made together. Thank you for the best 15 years of my life. I love you more than ever @pashapashkov. To many more laughs, travels, dances, cuddles, kisses, and babies,” she wrote. “Happy Anniversary, you’re my favorite. 1.1.09.”

Pashkov, for his part, also uploaded some photos of his best moments with Karagach. The DWTS pro even revealed a video of them dancing in a crowded street.

“15 years of dancing thru life together,” he wrote. “Laughing thru it, adventuring, growing, creating memories. I love you! I love us! Happy anniversary my love @daniellakaragach.”

Karagach and Pashkov kicked off their romance years before they became part of Dancing With The Stars. In 2009, the two were introduced by their dance coaches. They began dating in 2010, then they got engaged in 2012.

Two years later, the two tied the knot — five years before they joined DWTS for season 28. In May 30, 2023, the couple welcomed their baby girl. In a previous interview after her birth, Karagach stated that having Nikita further strengthened their romance.

“He’s literally the best father. And just watching him with her, holding our daughter, it just made me love him even more,” she said.

They Celebrated First Christmas with Their Daughter Nikita

Year 2023 marked the couple’s first Christmas with their daughter, a moment both DWTS pros consider special. On Instagram, Karagach shared how they celebrated the holidays.

The family of three wore matching red flannel onesies for Christmas. In the same post, Karagach also showed their personalized Christmas tree decorations. One is a pink onesie with text reading “Nikita’s 1st Christmas 2023” was written. Another is a flat Christmas ball-shaped decoration with “Our First Christmas as a Family of Three” was written.

A day later, the family celebrated Karagach’s birthday. In an Instagram post, Pashkov wrote a sweet long letter for his wife as tribute.

“Dani, we’ve been married for almost 10 years now and I think I know you inside out and yet you find ways to keep surprising and impressing me more. I always knew you are powerful, a superwoman, but recently I learned to which extent. You took on a demanding role of a mom and yet you did not miss a bit in any other area of your life,” he wrote.

Pashkov went on and expressed his adoration for Karagach, detailing how committed his wife is with both motherhood and her career.

“Thank you for being the best wife to me, thank you for being the best mom to Nikita. I’m so proud of you. I love you very much. WE love you very much!” he wrote.
