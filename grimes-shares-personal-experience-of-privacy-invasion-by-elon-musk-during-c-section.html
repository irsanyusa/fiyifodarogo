The latest revelation from singer Grimes sheds light on a deeply personal incident involving her former partner, Tesla CEO Elon Musk. In a recent biography on Musk, Grimes recounted an uncomfortable moment from her life, the time Musk sent around a photo of her undergoing a C-section during the birth of their first child.

Grimes Revealed Elon Musk Sent Photos of Her Having a C-Section

Grimes, whose real name is Claire Boucher, is no stranger to the spotlight. Known for her eclectic music and avant-garde style, she shares a life with one of the world’s most influential tech magnates, Musk. The couple has three children together: son X Æ A-Xii, daughter Exa Dark Sideræl, and their youngest son, known as Techno Mechanicus or “Tau.”

In a biography penned by writer Walter Isaacson, the singer revealed an unsettling incident involving Musk. During the birth of their first child, X Æ A-Xii, Musk took a photo of Grimes during her C-section. He then allegedly sent it to friends and family, including her father and siblings. Grimes, now 35, described Musk’s actions as “clueless” and expressed her bewilderment at his lack of understanding of why she would be upset by such a breach of privacy.

Grimes has been candid about her experience of co-parenting with Musk. She emphasizes her commitment to maintaining a healthy relationship with her children. In an interview with Wired, she discussed their efforts to determine the educational journey for their kids. Grimes expressed her determination to be both a disciplinarian and a friend to her children.

One intriguing tidbit from the interview is the revelation that their son, X Æ A-Xii, shares his father’s fascination with engineering and rocketry. Grimes humorously revealed that her son knows more about rockets than she does. She also would be thrilled if he followed in Musk’s engineering footsteps.

Grimes, Elon Musk Secretly Welcomed Their Third Child

The unconventional love story of billionaire entrepreneur Musk and musician Grimes continues to capture the public’s fascination. As previously reported, the couple secretly welcomed their third child, a son they affectionately refer to as “Tau.”

This event, disclosed in Musk’s forthcoming biography by journalist Walter Isaacson, offers a deeper look into Musk’s personal life, relationships, and the complexities of their family journey. The news of Mechanicus’s arrival comes from The New York Times. Which notes that Walter Isaacson has special access to Musk’s life over the past two years for his biography.

While Musk’s public image often revolves around his groundbreaking work in space exploration and electric vehicles, the book promises to reveal a more intimate side of the tech magnate. It highlights his struggles in building enduring relationships with his spouses, children, and collaborators, despite his remarkable achievements.
