What is Texas German and what does it sound like?

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1088 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Texas German describes a range of German dialects spoken in the US state of Texas. Germans were the largest ethnic group of immigrants from Europe to arrive in Texas as early as the 1830s. Many Germans settled in the so-called “German Belt” across the south-central portion of the state, and the area sprouted a number of unique language dialects and examples of German heritage in Texas. Full or partial German ancestry remains strong in Texas, and Germans form the third-largest national origin group in the state. [Read More]
