Kim Kardashian Receives Criticism for Saying That Being a Boy Mom Is Better Because Girls Have

 Posted on July 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  378 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Parenting is a demanding task, and it becomes even more challenging when you’re doing it as a single parent. Celebrities like Kim Kardashian, known for her business acumen and socialite status, also encounter these difficulties. Kardashian has shared her thoughts on parenting, but the public has criticized some of her statements.

Kim Kardashian became a single mother following her divorce from Kanye West.

Kim Kardashian filed for divorce from her former husband, Kanye West, in 2021, and their divorce was officially completed a year later. They have four children together: daughters North and Chicago and sons Saint and Psalm. While they had joint custody, sources close to Kardashian claim she took on most childcare responsibilities.

She’s raising 4 kids independently and has been candid about her challenges.

The well-known mom shared her experiences raising her 4 children as a single parent. She participated in a podcast where she expressed that solo parenting has been the “most challenging thing” for her. She admitted to having nights where she cried herself to sleep due to the varying moods and personalities of her children. “They’re fighting, and you know, there’s no one there. Like, it’s (up to) me to play good cop and bad cop,” she shared.

She faced criticism after expressing her views on parenting.

During one episode of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, Kim made a statement, saying, “I love my girls, but a girl steals your clothes and has an attitude, y’know? There’s nothing like being a boy mom, like, seriously, it’s the best.” This comment sparked a backlash from viewers criticizing Kim for favoring one gender over the other among her children.

This incident isn’t the first time Kim has made a controversial statement. In one of the previous episodes, when meeting with her pregnant sister Kylie, she made another controversial remark, saying, “The girls take the beauty to give it to themselves. The boys know what’s up, and they let you be a little prettier.” These comments have generated criticism and controversy among the show’s audience.

Nevertheless, Kardashian finds immense joy in the rewarding aspects of parenthood.

Despite the difficulties that accompany motherhood, Kardashian emphasizes the deep sense of fulfillment she gains from raising her children. She emphasizes that parenting is “the most rewarding job in the entire world,” and the experience has taught her much about herself.

Becoming a single mom is undoubtedly a challenging journey. Some celebrities who have chosen this path serve as a great source of inspiration. It’s heartening to follow their journeys and witness the growth of their children, who sometimes decide to follow in the footsteps of their famous parents.
