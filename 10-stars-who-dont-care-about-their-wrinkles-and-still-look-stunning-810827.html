10+ Stars Who Dont Care About Their Wrinkles and Still Look Stunning

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  744 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Many people think that celebrities use the services of plastic surgeons more often than other people. It’s because in their line of work, appearance is often a guarantee of success.

It’s not surprising that stars do everything in their power to keep their faces young and beautiful. But even among stars, there are some women that treat age changes as something normal, and they are openly against all sorts of “enhancements.” This article is about these women.

Keira Knightley, 37 years old

Hahn Lionel/ABACA/Abaca/East News

In one of her interviews, Keira said that she’s inspired by women with silver hair and wrinkles. The actress said she wanted to age naturally and look like these women when she got older. But at the same time, she worries when she finds new silver hair on her head and wants to dye them as soon as possible.

Keira is sure that she’s just scared of seeing a different person in the mirror. But, judging by her photos, she’s not planning to change anything about her appearance. And we support her decisions because she only gets more and more beautiful with age.

Anne Hathaway, 40 years old

Piovanotto Marco/ABACA/Abaca/East News

The star of The Devil Wears Prada thinks that wrinkles make women more beautiful.

Anne once said, “I had someone offer me Botox at 23. From the age of 25 to 27 — and this is a little embarrassing to admit — I looked under my eyes every single day, because I thought, ’It’s going, it’s going, it’s going!’ And then I realized the other day they’re not wrinkles, they’re smile lines. If you look at pictures of me from a young age, I’ve been smiling the same way since I was little. And I had that wonderful moment when you realize, ’That’s who I am.’ Botox would erase that.”

Carey Mulligan, 37 years old

ROBYN BECK/AFP/East News

Carey had a similar experience. The actress went to a clinic to get rid of small wrinkles around her eyes, and hoped they would recommend some cream. But they offered to inject a bit of Botox instead, and she refused. Her entire job is about moving her face.

Julianne Moore, 62 years old

Invision/Invision/East News

Julianne is not someone who judges others for using Botox. But she doesn’t think that injections are good for the appearance. She believes that Botox makes it obvious that a person did something to their face, and it often looks very strange.

Kirsten Dunst, 40 years old

LISA O'CONNOR/AFP/East News

Kirsten is convinced that Botox injections freeze facial expressions. And if she wants to build a successful career and appear in historical movies, she shouldn’t use Botox.

The star says, “I’ve heard of men doing it too, and I get so distracted by it. I can’t even see their performance because I’m just thinking, ‘What did they do to their face?’ ‘Filling’ is definitely the worst thing people do — it makes everyone look puffy.”

Salma Hayek, 56 years old

Evan Agostini/Invision/East News

The actress says that she’s never had any Botox injections. She seriously considered it and almost tried, but then changed her mind. Salma thinks that beauty is inside us, and women are beautiful at any age.

Kate Winslet, 47 years old

Marechal Aurore/ABACA/Abaca/East News

Kate says that she has no desire to reverse the aging process. She loves her wrinkles and takes very good care of her skin, using the necessary beauty products.

Jamie Lee Curtis, 64 years old

JUAN PABLO RICO/AFP/East News

Jamie is sure that aging is just as natural as a baby’s smooth skin. She says, “Aging is human evolution in its pure form.”

Jennifer Connelly, 52 years old

Kristin Callahan/Everett Collection/East News

The star of A Beautiful Mind values happiness and well-being more than anything else. Jennifer said that she’s not ready to torture herself with different procedures just to comply with the standards of society.

Heidi Klum, 49 years old

Collin Xavier/Image Press Agency ABACA/Abaca/East News

According to Heidi, plastic surgery creates an irreversible change in people’s faces. In most cases, it’s not good for them, especially for young girls. The model shares, “And I know girls half my age who do it. What are they gonna do at 40 or 50, when they really need it?”

Diane Lane, 57 years old

PATRICK T. FALLON/AFP/East News

Diane honestly says that she sometimes worries about her wrinkles, puffiness, and saggy skin. But she thinks that these changes are natural. Sooner or later, they happen to everyone, and it’s normal.

Christy Turlington, 53 years old

Evan Agostini/Invision/East News

Christy honestly says she doesn’t want to look younger than she really is. The supermodel says, “Our face is a map of our life — the more that’s there, the better.”

Jennifer Lopez, 53 years old

Invision/Invision/East News, Invision/Invision/East News

At the age of 53, Jennifer looks stunning. It’s not surprising that her appearance is widely discussed online.

Some people are sure that Lopez has had some injections. But she says she has never had any Botox, and she’s sad when people accuse her of lies. We believe her. In some photos, you can clearly see her age, which is beautiful.

What’s your attitude to anti-aging treatments? Have you ever had any?
