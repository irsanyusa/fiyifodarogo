Dwayne Michael Carter Jr., popularly known as Lil Wayne, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record executive and entrepreneur. Beyond his music career, Lil Wayne’s personal life has often been a subject of fascination among his fans. For instance, does Lil Wayne have a wife?
