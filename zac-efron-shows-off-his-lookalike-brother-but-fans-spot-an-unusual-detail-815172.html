Zac Efron Shows Off His Lookalike Brother, but Fans Spot an Unusual Detail

 Posted on August 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  355 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Zac Efron is an absolute dreamboat, but just wait till you see his brother, as good looks certainly run in their family. To our absolute delight, the High School Musical star recently shared some shirtless pics on a day out with his rarely-seen younger brother, and our eyes went to heaven. Curiously, however, Zac’s fans were distracted by something other than their chiseled abs, and now we can’t unsee it either.

A quick guide to the Efron siblings.

Zac Efron is the oldest one in his family, and he takes his big brother role very seriously. Closest in age, is his brother Dylan, who describes their relationship as quite competitive in the past, but very loving today: “We were always fighting with each other... And then once I graduated from college Zac really became that older brother, and that’s when I would say we grew closer than ever.”

The former Disney star also has the cutest little half-siblings: sister Olivia and brother Henry, both on his father’s side. Efron often posts pics with his baby siblings, and even once made all of his fans jealous by announcing that little Olivia was his Valentine. Absolutely adorable beyond belief.

Zac and Dylan enjoyed a bro day out.

The Efron brothers decided to close out the summer in style and packed all warm weather activities they could in one day. On August 31, Zac and Dylan seemingly enjoyed an outdoor getaway, that included a boat, some swimming, and golfing, as we gathered from the actor’s Instagram post.

The two siblings really showed off their likeness as they were twinning with their impressive abs. In addition, they reminded us of all the fun things we’re going to miss this summer, so we definitely appreciate the photo report Zac shared with everyone.

Fans got hyperfocused on one specific thing.

As expected, Efron’s comment section blew up with overjoyed fans, but they were distracted by something we couldn’t have predicted. Most people became nostalgic over Zac’s role as Troy Bolton in High School Musical 2, in which he had an iconic singing scene on the golf course.

So we’re guessing it’s time to rewatch the High School Musical movies! Fitting, considering the school year just started.

We’re also extra happy to see Zac out and about, considering a very concerning health scare he had to deal with a few years ago. Efron suffered a horrible injury, which, unfortunately, changed the way his face looks forever, but we’re grateful that he’s still healthy and happy.
